Inside the Assessment

Your 5-Dimension Readiness Profile

Your results are built around five core dimensions — each a critical ingredient for meaningful, sustainable change. Together, they reveal where alignment already exists and where focus will create the greatest momentum.

01

Clarity & Self-Awareness

What it measures: Your understanding of who you are, what no longer fits, and who you are becoming.

Why it matters: Without clarity, effort is scattered. Transformation requires knowing what you're moving toward and why change is necessary.

02

Emotional Resilience & Adaptability

What it measures: Your ability to navigate uncertainty, self-doubt, and discomfort during change.

Why it matters: Reinvention is emotionally demanding. Resilience determines whether you move through a challenge or stop when things feel uncomfortable.

03

Support & Environment

What it measures: Whether your relationships, routines, and surroundings support your growth.

Why it matters: Even motivated individuals struggle in unsupportive environments. Context can accelerate or constrain progress.

04

Action Orientation & Follow-Through

What it measures: Your ability to take consistent action and turn intention into behavior.

Why it matters: Insight alone does not create change. Progress depends on execution, habits, and follow-through.

05

Purpose & Motivation

What it measures: The strength of your internal "why" for transformation.

Why it matters: Purpose fuels sustained effort. Without it, change feels heavy and short-lived.
