About Andrew

Andrew Asnes

Career Reinvention Coach · Transformation Strategist · Founder, Project Future Self

Most coaches talk about reinvention. Andrew Asnes has done it five times.

Principal Dancer with the Paul Taylor Dance Company. Broadway and Off-Broadway producer. Columbia MBA. XR startup co-founder (acquired). Accenture consultant. Chief Growth Officer at an AI-powered CX startup. Each pivot wasn't a fresh start — it was a deliberate reinvention, built on everything that came before and pointed toward something bigger.

That lived experience is the foundation of Project Future Self, a hands-on transformation system for executives, mid-career professionals, and entrepreneurs who are ready to stop managing their careers and start designing them.
