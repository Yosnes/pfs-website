DAY1
Day 1 · Clear Diagnosis
Diagnostic — Name the Real Problem

You can't fix a confidence problem you haven't clearly named. This day is about precise diagnosis — not venting, not catastrophizing. What is actually happening, and what does it cost you?

1
What happened that shook your confidence?
Be specific: what was the event, decision, or situation?
2
What do you think is really going on underneath?
The event is the surface. What's the root — fear of failure? Of not being enough? Of being exposed?
3
What pattern keeps showing up?
Avoidance? Overthinking? Withdrawal? Perfectionism? Something else?
★
What is this confidence erosion costing you right now?
Opportunities missed, relationships affected, momentum lost — make it real.
The Real Cost

Day 3 · Future Self Anchor
Identity — Build Your Anchor

Your Future Self anchor is a clear, evidence-based reference point for who you're becoming. Not who you hope to become — who you're already in the process of becoming. You'll return to this on hard days.

1
Describe your Future Self in the present tense
Who is the person you're becoming? Write as if they already exist.

"I am someone who ___. I show up as ___. I operate from ___."

2
Evidence this person already exists in you
List 3–5 specific moments, decisions, or qualities that prove this isn't wishful thinking.
