The Program

What Is the 5-Day Reinvention Sprint?

The 5-Day Reinvention Sprint is a live, small-group coaching workshop designed for professionals who are ready to stop waiting and start moving. Over five focused days, you'll work through a structured process that takes you from stuck and uncertain to clear, confident, and in motion.

This isn't a passive seminar or a pre-recorded course. Each day includes live coaching sessions, hands-on exercises, and a concrete action mission — so you leave every session with something real.

Small Group Format

Intimate cohort for real coaching, real feedback, real breakthroughs.

Daily Action Missions

Each session ends with a mission you complete before the next day — progress every 24 hours.

Tools You Keep Forever

Every framework, template, and blueprint is yours — designed for ongoing use long after the sprint ends.

90-Day Roadmap on Day 5

You leave the sprint with a personalized, executable roadmap — not a concept, a plan.

The Curriculum

The 5-Day Schedule

Each day is a live session + a daily action mission you complete before the next session.

Day

1

Where Am I Really?

Reinvention Matrix + Transformation Readiness Assessment

You can't navigate to where you're going if you don't know where you actually are. Day 1 breaks down the brutal, honest truth of your current position — professionally and personally. Using the Reinvention Matrix, you'll map your exact reinvention stage and identify the patterns that have been keeping you stuck.

Reinvention Matrix Readiness Diagnosis Daily Action Mission

Day

2

Who Am I Becoming?

Future-Self Identity Blueprint

Reinvention isn't just about changing what you do — it's about becoming who you need to be. Day 2 is devoted to building a vivid, specific, and executable vision of your Future Self. You'll create your Future-Self Identity Blueprint: the values, strengths, roles, and non-negotiables that define the person you're becoming.

Identity Blueprint Values Mapping Daily Action Mission

Day

3

How Do I Tell My Story?

Narrative Flip Model

The story you tell about yourself determines the opportunities you attract. Day 3 introduces the Narrative Flip Model — a framework for transforming your reinvention from a liability into your greatest asset. You'll craft a professional narrative that is honest, compelling, and positions your pivot as a strength.

Narrative Flip Model Story Workshop Daily Action Mission

Day

4

How Do I Turn This Into Opportunity?

Opportunity Engine Templates™

With clarity on who you are and where you're going, Day 4 shifts to offense. Using the Opportunity Engine Templates™, you'll identify where your reinvented identity creates the most leverage — in your current role, in the job market, or as an entrepreneur — and map out the specific moves to capitalize on it.

Opportunity Engine™ Leverage Mapping Daily Action Mission

Day

5

What Happens Next?

90-Day Reinvention Roadmap + Future Self OS™

Day 5 is about execution. You'll build your complete 90-Day Reinvention Roadmap — a real, milestone-driven plan covering the next 12 weeks. Then you'll configure your Future Self OS™: the personal operating system (habits, routines, decision frameworks) designed to sustain your momentum long after the sprint is over.
